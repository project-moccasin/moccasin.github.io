Project Moccasin

Sep. 22-26

Spend a day with a product or design mentor and see what it’s like to be in their shoes.

Who Should Attend

Designers (UX/UI, Interaction, Product) and Product Managers (junior to mid-level)

When

The next event takes place during the week of Sep. 22-26, 2014.

Where

Various mentor locations in New York City

About

Ever wonder what it’s like to build product in early-stage startup? Curious about how teams make product decisions? What’s it like to be a solo designer or a designer-founder?

Project Moccasin is an opportunity for designers and product folks to spend a day with a product or design mentor to observe how they work, where they work.

The Mentors

Mentors include designer-founders, product designers and product managers from early-stage, funded startups to mid-sized, growth-stage companies and agencies.

How it Works

  1. Apply to participate. We’ll follow up to ensure we have a mentor match for you and answer questions you might have.
  2. Reserve your spot. Once we have identified a match, pay the $35 fee to lock in your mentor. Early reservations get early selection of mentors.
  3. Pick a day. You and your mentor pick a date during Sep. 22-26 to spend a day where they work.
