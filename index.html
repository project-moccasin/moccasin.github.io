Currently accepting applications for New York, NY and Austin, TX.

Who Should Apply

Designers, product managers, makers…or anyone transitioning into those roles.

When

Application deadline: March 6, 2015. Shadowing takes place one day during March 23-April 3 in NYC and Austin.

Where

Various locations in New York, NY and Austin, TX

About

Ever wonder what it’s like to build product in early-stage startups? Curious about how teams make product decisions?Â What’s it like to be a solo designer or a founder?

Project Moccasin is an opportunity for creative and entrepreneurial folks to spend a day with a product, entrepreneurial or design mentor to observe how they work, where they work.

The Mentors

Past mentors included product designers, makers and product managers from startups and collectives like Alphaworks and Maker’s Row, to growth-stage companies like Shapeways, Kickstarter, and Etsy to name a few.

How it Works

  1. Apply to participate. We’ll follow up to ensure we have a mentor match for you and answer questions you might have. Application deadline is Friday, March 6, 2015.
  2. Reserve your spot. Once we have identified a match, make your $40 payment to lock in your mentor. Early reservations have the best chance of matching with the limited number of participating mentors.
  3. Pick a day. You and your mentor agree on a day during March 23-April 3 to spend the day where they work.
